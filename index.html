Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym timings?
We are open from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Sunday is 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
Is Personal Training included?
Personal training is included in the Platinum plan. For other plans, it is available as a separate add-on.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 1 month in case of travel or medical issues (requires prior notice).
